# Operational Risk & Revenue Leakage Impact Analytics

## Project Overview

This project analyzes operational risk incidents and their financial impact on organizational revenue. The objective is to identify revenue leakage patterns, evaluate operational loss impact, and provide insights for improving risk management and financial planning.

## Business Problem

Organizations often experience financial losses due to operational failures, process inefficiencies, or risk incidents. Without proper analysis, these losses remain unnoticed and continue to impact profitability. This project aims to analyze operational risk events and measure their contribution to revenue leakage.

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains operational risk records including:

* Record ID
* Region
* Risk Category
* Severity Level
* Root Cause
* Revenue Leakage Amount
* Operational Loss Impact


The dataset was cleaned and prepared for analysis using Excel and Power BI.

## Tools & Technologies

* Microsoft Excel
* Power BI
* Data Cleaning and Transformation
* Data Visualization
* Business Intelligence Analytics

## Dashboard Features

The Power BI dashboard provides the following insights:

* Planned Operational Budget vs Actual Operational Spend
* Budget Variance Analysis
* Operational Loss Impact
* High Risk Incident Count
* Monthly Revenue Leakage Trend
* Revenue Leakage Distribution by Risk Category
* Revenue Impact by Severity Level
* Risk Incident Distribution by Region
* Geographic Visualization of Revenue Leakage
* Interactive Filters for Date, Region, Severity Level, Risk Category, and Root Cause

## Key Insights

* High severity incidents contribute the most to revenue leakage.
* Operational spend exceeded the planned budget resulting in a negative variance.
* Certain regions show higher concentrations of operational risk incidents.
* Monthly analysis reveals fluctuations in revenue leakage trends.
